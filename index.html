I am an incoming first-year Ph.D. student in Computer Science at the University of Maryland (UMD), advised by Professor Abhinav Shrivastava.

My research interests include deep learning and human-computer interaction for domain-specific reasoning and human intention monitoring. I am particularly interested in applying this line of research to sports analytics and biomechanics.

I completed my B.S. in Computer Science at UMD College Park, where I was fortunate to work with Professor Abhinav Shrivastava and Professor Jae K. Shim.
